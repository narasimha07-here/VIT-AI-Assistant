=== CLUBS & CHAPTERS AT VIT-AP ===

VIT-AP University promotes holistic student development by offering numerous opportunities to learn, lead, and collaborate beyond academics.  
With 70+ active clubs and chapters, students can explore technical innovation, arts, cultural heritage, professional growth, and social responsibility.
